Here Tyrion is enjoying some love from his favorite person. Early in the breed's history they were used by the German Army, but Tyrion, though surely capable, is a sweet lapdog (AKC.org)

Shnauzers use their long whiskers for protection, sometimes even from their own stuffed friends. But traditionally, when they matted it would protect them from vermin bites if they were useds to hunt on farms (AKC.org)

As you might suspect, Schnauzers are named for a German word which means "snout," though they used to be called Wirehaired Pinschers the new term replaced their original breed name in 1990's (AKC.org).
